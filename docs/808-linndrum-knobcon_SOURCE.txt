HISTORICAL CONTEXT: The Roland TR-808 (1980) is arguably the most influential drum machine ever made — its booming bass drum and crispy hi-hats defined hip-hop, electro, house, and modern pop. The LinnDrum (1982) was the first drum machine to use digital samples of real drums and was used extensively in 80s pop, new wave, and R&B (Prince, Peter Gabriel, The Human League).
