## Context

P.O.S appeared **7 times in Tiger Dust**, plus Doomtree (2x) and SHREDDERS (2x) = 11 combined Minneapolis hip-hop appearances. This is the second-biggest unmapped cluster, now formalized as the **Minneapolis Machine** bank.

P.O.S is known for complex production: odd time signatures, noise layers over boom-bap, experimental drum programming, and crew-based collaboration (Doomtree collective). This bank needs sounds that support that aesthetic.

## Target Sounds

### Category 1: Boom-Bap Drum Kits
- Dusty, vinyl-sourced kicks and snares
- MPC-style chopped breaks
- Lo-fi, compressed drum one-shots
- Swing-heavy programmed patterns
- **Character:** Dusty, warm, analog. NOT trap or modern 808.
- **Reference:** P.O.S, DJ Premier, Madlib

### Category 2: Experimental / Odd-Meter Drums
- Polyrhythmic percussion loops
- Odd time signature patterns (5/4, 7/8, 6/4)
- Layered complex drum programming
- Drum patterns that shift or evolve
- **Character:** Intentionally off-grid, complex, unpredictable
- **Reference:** P.O.S (sleepdrone/superposition), Doomtree

### Category 3: Noise Textures / Glitch
- Industrial noise layers
- Tape hiss, vinyl crackle, analog warmth textures
- Glitch and stutter effects (crossover with Brat Mode)
- Radio static, electromagnetic interference
- **Character:** Textural, atmospheric, used as production layers
- **Reference:** P.O.S, Dalek, Death Grips (adjacent)

### Category 4: Synth Bass / Sub
- Gritty synth bass (not clean 808)
- Distorted mid-range fuzz bass
- Dark, heavy sub with character
- **Character:** Dirty, present, mid-heavy
- **Reference:** Run The Jewels, SHREDDERS

### Category 5: Vocal Chops / Rap Textures
- Chopped vocal syllables for rhythmic use
- Processed vocal textures (pitched, reversed, granular)
- Spoken word fragments
- **Character:** Used as percussion/texture, not melody
- **Reference:** P.O.S vocal delivery style

### Category 6: Ambient / Dark Pads
- Dark atmospheric drones
- Industrial ambient textures
- Eerie, menacing pad sounds
- **Character:** Sparse, tension-building, minimal
- **Reference:** Doomtree production, Massive Attack (adjacent)

## Search Strategy

### Free Sources to Prioritize
- **r/drumkits** — boom-bap and underground hip-hop kits are well-represented here
- **Looperman** — hip-hop tagged loops, boom-bap beats
- **MusicRadar SampleRadar** — check hip-hop, experimental, EBM archives
- **Freesound.org** — noise textures, tape hiss, vinyl crackle (CC0)
- **SampleFocus** — search: boom-bap, dusty, lo-fi, experimental
- **Ghosthack** — check experimental and electronic sections
- **Cymatics** — has hip-hop oriented packs in free vault

### Specialty Sources
- **Splice** — if subscription available, search "boom bap" and "underground hip hop"
- **99Sounds** — check for abstract/experimental packs
- **Legowelt** — Drum Wizardry pack has vintage electronic drums that cross over

### Important Notes
- Boom-bap drums are well-served in the free sample ecosystem (unlike punk)
- Noise/glitch textures overlap with Brat Mode sources — deduplicate against existing downloads
- Odd-meter content is rare — may need to search for "experimental percussion" or "polyrhythmic"
- The Doomtree/Minneapolis aesthetic is specific — it's not generic "underground hip-hop"
