## Target Sounds

The Riot Mode bank needs:

### Category 1: Punk Drum Kits
- Fast punk kick/snare (tight, cracked, trashy)
- Blast beats and double-time fills
- Loose hi-hats, crash-heavy cymbal patterns
- Ska-style snare on every beat
- **Character:** Raw, loud, room-mic energy. NOT polished metal drums.
- **Reference:** The Interrupters, Rancid, RVIVR

### Category 2: Distorted Guitar Samples
- Power chord stabs (E, A, D — standard punk tunings)
- Palm-muted chugging patterns
- Ska upstroke clean rhythm guitar
- Feedback, screech, noise textures
- **Character:** Marshall amp crunch, not modern metal gain
- **Reference:** The Distillers, Le Tigre, Sleigh Bells

### Category 3: Ska Brass / Horn Stabs
- Ska horn section stabs (trumpet, trombone, sax hits)
- Horn melody lines
- Brass ensemble bursts
- **Character:** Tight, punchy, Jamaican-influenced ska-punk
- **Reference:** The Interrupters, The Soviettes, Less Than Jake

### Category 4: Bass Lines
- Punk driving bass (fuzz, distorted, fast eighth notes)
- Ska walking bass lines
- Picked bass with grit
- **Character:** Mid-heavy, cutting through guitar, aggressive
- **Reference:** Rancid (Matt Freeman), RVIVR

### Category 5: Vocal Samples / Chants
- Gang vocal shouts ("hey!", "oi!", "go!")
- Call-and-response patterns
- Female vocal shouts and screams
- Spoken word / political speech snippets (royalty-free)
- **Character:** Raw, passionate, unpolished
- **Reference:** Le Tigre, Pussy Riot, The Soviettes

### Category 6: Noise / Feedback / Texture
- Guitar feedback swells
- Amp hum and buzz
- Venue/room ambience (punk shows)
- Tape distortion, lo-fi recording artifacts
- **Character:** DIY aesthetic, intentionally rough
- **Reference:** Sleigh Bells, Crystal Castles (crossover)

## Search Strategy

### Free Sources to Prioritize
- **MusicRadar SampleRadar** — search archive for punk, ska, rock drum kits
- **Freesound.org** — CC0 punk drum hits, guitar feedback, crowd recordings
- **r/drumkits** — community punk and rock drum kits
- **Looperman** — punk/rock tagged loops and one-shots
- **Bedroom Producers Blog** — occasionally features rock/punk sample packs
- **SampleFocus** — search tags: punk, rock, distorted, ska, brass

### Specialty Sources
- **99Sounds** — check for rock/noise packs
- **Cymatics** — unlikely to have punk but check rock section
- **Ghosthack** — check experimental section for noise/distortion textures

### Important Notes
- Punk samples are LESS common in free sample pack ecosystems (which skew electronic/hip-hop)
- May need to look at rock/indie sample packs and filter for the aggressive end
- Ska brass is particularly rare in free packs — may need to search for general brass one-shots and process them
- Guitar DI samples that can be re-amped are valuable even without distortion
