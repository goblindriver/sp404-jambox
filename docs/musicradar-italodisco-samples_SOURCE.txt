HISTORICAL CONTEXT: Italo disco originated in Italy in the late 1970s as a synth-heavy, electronic variant of disco. Key artists include Giorgio Moroder, Baltimora, Den Harrow, Kano. Characterized by heavy use of synthesizers, drum machines, and vocoders. Hugely influential on house music, Hi-NRG, and Eurobeat. Artists like Little Boots, Chromeo, and LCD Soundsystem have drawn heavily from the italo disco template.
