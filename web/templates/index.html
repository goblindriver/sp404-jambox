SP-404 JAMBOX

1

Describe your sounds

Click any pad to describe what you want on it. Each bank has a genre and key — pads 1-4 are drum hits, 5-12 are loops and melodic content.

2

Fetch samples

Hit Fetch All to search your local library + Freesound.org for matches. Or click a pad and hit Fetch for just that one.

3

Ingest new packs

Drop sample packs into ~/Downloads and hit Ingest Downloads. RAR archives get extracted, WAVs get categorized into your library automatically.

4

Build & deploy

Hit Build to generate PAD_INFO.BIN and starter patterns. Then insert your SD card and hit Deploy to SD.

5

Jam & save your gold

Bank A is yours — resample and chop on-device. When you plug the card back in, your Bank A edits get saved to the gold library.
