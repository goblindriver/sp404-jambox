SP-404 JAMBOX

1

Inspire a bank

Click the ✦ Inspire button next to a bank name. The LLM picks a genre, BPM, key, and description for you — or type your own in the edit modal.

2

Draft pad descriptions

Press the sparkle button next to Inspire (same icon) to let the LLM write pad descriptions from the bank’s metadata. Review and tweak them if you like.

3

Fetch samples

Hit the download icon to search your library for matches. Need all banks at once? Find Fetch All Banks in Settings.

4

Export & jam

Hit Export to SD to build PAD_INFO and deploy. Bank A is yours — resample and chop on-device. Plug the card back in and tap the header sparkle (same icon as Inspire) to pull card intelligence: PAD_INFO tweaks, pattern usage, and bed context.

5

Grow your library

Drop sample packs into ~/Downloads and choose Ingest Downloads from Settings. Archives get extracted and categorized automatically.
